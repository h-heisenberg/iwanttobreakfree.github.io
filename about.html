About Heisenberg Labs

Pioneering 3D printing and advanced manufacturing in Nepal since our founding. We're on a mission to make cutting-edge technology accessible to innovators, businesses, and creators across the country.

Our Story

Heisenberg Labs was founded with a vision to revolutionize manufacturing in Nepal. We recognized the growing need for rapid prototyping, custom manufacturing, and engineering R&D services in Kathmandu and across the country.

What started as a small 3D printing service has grown into Nepal's most comprehensive additive manufacturing and engineering solutions provider. We've helped hundreds of clients — from individual inventors to large enterprises — bring their ideas to life.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art facility in Kathmandu, equipped with the latest 3D printing technology and staffed by experienced engineers and technicians who are passionate about innovation.

Our Mission

To empower innovators, businesses, medical institutions and creators in Nepal with world-class 3D printing and engineering R&D services. We strive to make advanced manufacturing technology accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone.

Our Vision

To establish Nepal as a regional hub for additive manufacturing and innovation, fostering a culture of creativity, technical excellence, and sustainable manufacturing practices.
